My Favourite Movie

Harry Potter Movies

Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry Potter, an eleven-year-old orphan, discovers that he is a wizard and is invited to study at Hogwarts. Even as he escapes a dreary life and enters a world of magic, he finds trouble awaiting him. Read more






Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets

A house elf warns Harry against returning to Hogwarts, but he decides to ignore it. When students and creatures at the school begin to get petrified, Harry finds himself surrounded in mystery. Read more






Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban

Harry Potter learns that Sirius Black has escaped from the prison of Azkaban and is planning to kill him. Meanwhile, Hagrid is distraught when his hippogriff, Buckbeak, is sentenced to death. Read more






Harry Potter and the Goblet of Fire

In his fourth year at Hogwarts, Harry is unwittingly selected to compete in the inter-school Triwizard Tournament. Meanwhile, the wizarding world remains unaware of the ominous rise of dark forces. Read more






Harry Potter and the Order of the Phoenix

Harry Potter and Dumbledore's warning about the return of Lord Voldemort is not heeded by the wizard authorities who, in turn, look to undermine Dumbledore's authority at Hogwarts and discredit Harry. Read more






Harry Potter and the Half-Blood Prince

Dumbledore and Harry Potter learn more about Voldemort's past and his rise to power. Meanwhile, Harry stumbles upon an old potions textbook belonging to a person calling himself the Half-Blood Prince. Read more






Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part I

After Voldemort takes over the Ministry of Magic, Harry, Ron and Hermione are forced into hiding. They try to decipher the clues left to them by Dumbledore to find and destroy Voldemort's Horcruxes. Read more






Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows - Part 2

Harry, Ron and Hermione race against time to destroy the remaining Horcruxes. Meanwhile, the students and teachers unite to defend Hogwarts against Lord Voldemort and the Death Eaters. Read more
